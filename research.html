Working Paper

South Korean Engagement Strategies and North Korean Military Provocations: A Quantitative Analysis (1998-2025)

This research examines the relationship between South Korean economic aid and North Korean military provocations across six presidential administrations. Using quantitative methods, I analyze patterns of engagement and provocation to understand how policy approaches influence security outcomes on the Korean Peninsula.
